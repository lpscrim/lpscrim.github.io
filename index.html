ARTIST PROFILE

LEWIS SCRIMGEOUR

Lewis Scrimgeour is an Artist from Dunfermline, Scotland and spends most of the time creating, travelling or talking shite.
Lewis has interests in capturing sensory treats from around the world and using them in creative experiments.
Fascination with organic shapes and patterns combined and a love of travel are often the starting point for many works.
